# IRENA Search

Search and reference key IRENA (International Renewable Energy Agency) publications, reports, and policy documents.

## Instructions

When the user asks about IRENA reports, renewable energy statistics, policy frameworks, or global energy transition data:

1. Search the built-in index of key IRENA publications below.
2. Provide the publication title, year, and a brief summary.
3. Include the direct URL to the publication when available.
4. If the user asks about specific data points, reference the relevant publication and provide the data.
5. For topics not covered in the index, suggest the user visit the IRENA publications portal.

## Publication Index

### Renewable Power Generation Costs in 2023
- Year: 2024
- URL: https://www.irena.org/Publications/2024/Sep/Renewable-Power-Generation-Costs-in-2023
- Summary: Comprehensive analysis of renewable power generation costs worldwide. Reports global weighted-average LCOE of $0.049/kWh for solar PV (down 90% since 2010), $0.033/kWh for onshore wind, and $0.075/kWh for offshore wind.

### World Energy Transitions Outlook 2024
- Year: 2024
- URL: https://www.irena.org/Publications/2024/Sep/World-Energy-Transitions-Outlook-2024
- Summary: Flagship report on the global energy transition pathway to 1.5°C. Calls for tripling renewable capacity to 11 TW by 2030 and $35 trillion in annual energy investment.

### Renewable Capacity Statistics 2024
- Year: 2024
- URL: https://www.irena.org/Publications/2024/Mar/Renewable-capacity-statistics-2024
- Summary: Comprehensive dataset of renewable energy capacity by country and technology. Reports global renewable capacity reached 3,870 GW at end of 2023, with 473 GW added in 2023 alone.

### Global Landscape of Renewable Energy Finance 2023
- Year: 2023
- URL: https://www.irena.org/Publications/2023/Feb/Global-landscape-of-renewable-energy-finance-2023
- Summary: Analysis of renewable energy investment flows. Reports $499 billion invested in renewable energy in 2022, with 70% concentrated in China, EU, US, and India.

### Geopolitics of the Energy Transformation
- Year: 2022
- URL: https://www.irena.org/Publications/2022/Jan/Geopolitics-of-the-Energy-Transformation
- Summary: Analysis of how the energy transition reshapes geopolitical dynamics, trade patterns, and international relations. Highlights shift from fuel-exporting to technology-exporting nations.

### Renewable Energy and Jobs Annual Review 2023
- Year: 2023
- URL: https://www.irena.org/Publications/2023/Sep/Renewable-energy-and-jobs-Annual-review-2023
- Summary: Reports 13.7 million jobs in renewable energy globally in 2022. Solar PV leads with 4.9 million jobs, followed by bioenergy (3.6M), hydropower (2.5M), and wind (1.4M).

### Off-Grid Renewable Energy Statistics 2023
- Year: 2023
- URL: https://www.irena.org/Publications/2023/Jun/Off-grid-renewable-energy-statistics-2023
- Summary: Data on off-grid renewable energy deployment. Reports 180 million people served by off-grid solar, with mini-grids serving 18 million people across 14,000+ installations.

### Green Hydrogen Cost Reduction
- Year: 2020
- URL: https://www.irena.org/Publications/2020/Dec/Green-hydrogen-cost-reduction
- Summary: Roadmap for reducing green hydrogen costs from $3–6/kg to below $1.5/kg by 2050 through electrolyser cost reduction and cheap renewable electricity.

### Renewable Energy Auctions: Status and Trends Beyond Price
- Year: 2023
- URL: https://www.irena.org/Publications/2023/Mar/Renewable-energy-auctions-Status-and-trends-beyond-price
- Summary: Analysis of renewable energy auction mechanisms worldwide. Documents how auctions have driven down costs and attracted $250+ billion in investment since 2010.

### Planning and Prospects for Renewable Power: Africa
- Year: 2023
- URL: https://www.irena.org/Publications/2023/Jan/Planning-and-prospects-for-renewable-power-Africa
- Summary: Africa-focused analysis of renewable energy potential and planning. Identifies 7,900 GW of solar and 460 GW of wind technical potential across the continent.

## Examples

**User:** What does IRENA say about solar PV costs?

**Assistant:** According to IRENA's "Renewable Power Generation Costs in 2023" report (published September 2024):

The global weighted-average LCOE for solar PV fell to **$0.049/kWh** in 2023, representing a **90% decline since 2010**. Key findings include:

| Metric | 2010 | 2023 | Change |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| Solar PV LCOE | $0.460/kWh | $0.049/kWh | -89% |
| Onshore Wind LCOE | $0.107/kWh | $0.033/kWh | -69% |
| Offshore Wind LCOE | $0.197/kWh | $0.075/kWh | -62% |

Solar PV is now the cheapest source of new electricity generation in most markets worldwide. In 2023, 87% of newly commissioned utility-scale solar PV had lower costs than the cheapest new fossil fuel alternative.

Full report: https://www.irena.org/Publications/2024/Sep/Renewable-Power-Generation-Costs-in-2023
